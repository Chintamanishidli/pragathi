Who We Are

Our Story

Born from a love of beautiful spaces, we've been transforming homes and apartments into extraordinary environments since 2016.

Our Beginning

Transforming Spaces,
Elevating Lives

Pragathi Home Solutions was founded in 2016 by Priya Sharma, a Bengaluru-based architect with an eye for minimalist grandeur. What began as a two-person boutique firm has blossomed into a 45-member team of passionate designers, architects, and craftspeople specializing in Interior Designing Service For Home, Apartments, and Luxury Steel Kitchen Interiors.

We believe every space tells a story — and our kitchen is the heart of that story. From bespoke stainless steel kitchens that resist moisture, heat, and time, to complete home interiors that reflect refined living — we work hand-in-hand with our clients to craft spaces that are impeccably built and visually captivating.
