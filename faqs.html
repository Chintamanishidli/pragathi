Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Pragathi Home Solutions.

General Questions

What services does Pragathi Home Solutions offer?
We offer a full spectrum of interior design services including residential and commercial design, space planning, 3D visualization, furniture sourcing, project management, Vastu consultation, and renovation management. Whether you need a single room styled or a full-floor corporate fit-out, we have the expertise to handle it.
Absolutely. We have completed projects across India — including Mumbai, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Delhi — as well as international commissions. We work remotely where needed and travel for site visits at key project milestones.
Can you work with my existing furniture and décor?
Of course! We often work around pieces you love. During the initial consultation, we assess your existing furniture and décor and build a design plan that complements what you already have while elevating the overall aesthetic.

Pricing

How is the design fee structured?
Our fees vary depending on project scope. We offer three models: (1) Fixed Fee — ideal for smaller, well-defined projects; (2) Per Square Foot — for residential and commercial renovations; (3) Percentage of Total Project Cost — commonly used for luxury and high-budget projects. We provide a transparent quotation after the first consultation.
What is the minimum project budget you accept?
We typically accept residential projects with a minimum design budget of ₹5 lakhs and a furnishing/execution budget of ₹15 lakhs and above. For commercial projects, the minimum starts at ₹25 lakhs. We believe in delivering quality over volume, so we take on select projects where we can make a meaningful impact.
Is the initial consultation free?
Yes! Your first 60-minute consultation — either in-person at our studio or via video call — is completely free. This session allows us to understand your vision, assess your space, and give you an initial idea of what we can achieve together.

Our Process

What is your design process from start to finish?
Our proven 6-step process includes: Discovery & Brief (understanding your lifestyle and aspirations) → Concept Development (mood boards, style direction) → Design Proposal (floor plans, 3D renders, material palettes) → Revision & Approval → Execution & Procurement → Styling & Handover. We keep you involved at every stage.
Will I have a dedicated point of contact throughout my project?
Yes. Every client is assigned a dedicated Project Manager who coordinates between the design team, contractors, and vendors. You'll have a single point of contact for updates, queries, and escalations throughout the project lifecycle.

Timeline

How long does a typical interior design project take?
Timeline depends on project size and complexity. A single room typically takes 4–6 weeks. A full apartment or villa can take 3–6 months. Large commercial projects may run 6–12 months. We provide a detailed project schedule at the outset and update it regularly so you always know where things stand.
What if my project gets delayed due to material supply issues?
We proactively manage supply chains and work with trusted local vendors to minimise delays. In cases where specific materials face supply disruptions, we propose equivalent alternatives promptly so your project stays on track. We maintain transparent communication about any changes to the schedule.

Materials & Sustainability

Do you offer eco-friendly or sustainable design options?
Sustainability is at the heart of our practice. We source FSC-certified wood, low-VOC paints, recycled metals, and locally-crafted furniture to reduce our carbon footprint. We help clients earn green building ratings and can design entirely around an eco-conscious brief if desired.
Where do you source your furniture and materials from?
We work with a curated network of Indian artisans, premium furniture brands (both domestic and international), and specialty material suppliers. We prioritize quality craftsmanship and unique pieces — you won't find generic catalogue items in our projects. We also provide complete transparency on pricing with no hidden markups beyond our standard procurement fee.

Steel Kitchen Questions

Why invest in a stainless steel kitchen over a wooden one?
A stainless steel kitchen is a premium long-term investment — it is termite-proof, waterproof, fire-resistant, and incredibly durable compared to wooden kitchens. Steel surfaces are non-porous, hygienic, and resist bacterial growth, making them the standard choice in professional kitchens worldwide. Unlike wood, steel does not warp, swell, or degrade over time, ensuring your kitchen retains its elegance for decades.
Is a stainless steel kitchen suitable for my home?
Absolutely. Our steel kitchen designs are tailored specifically to bring a refined, premium aesthetic to modern residential homes. Far from the utilitarian look of commercial kitchens, our bespoke steel interiors incorporate warm textures, soft lighting, and elegant hardware to create spaces that are both luxurious and deeply personal to your home.
Can my steel kitchen design be fully personalized?
Every kitchen we create is fully customized to reflect your space, preferences, and lifestyle. We offer L-shaped, U-shaped, Island, and bespoke layouts. Finishes, hardware, countertop materials, storage configurations, and lighting schemes are all chosen collaboratively with you to ensure the result is a true expression of your vision.
Is a steel kitchen difficult to maintain?
Not at all. Our kitchens are specifically engineered for effortless upkeep, retaining their brilliance and elegance with minimal care. A simple wipe-down with a damp cloth is often all that's needed. The non-porous surface resists stains and grease, making it far easier to maintain than tiled, wooden, or laminated kitchen surfaces.
How long does a steel kitchen installation take?
From design consultation to final installation, timelines are efficiently managed to ensure a seamless experience. Typically, a complete steel kitchen project takes 2–4 weeks depending on the complexity, size, and any customization involved. We provide a precise timeline at project commencement and update you regularly throughout the process.
